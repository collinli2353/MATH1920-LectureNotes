# Plane
Planes need:
- point + normal [[vector]]
- three non-collinear points

## Vector Form
$$n \cdot \langle x,y,z \rangle = 0$$, where $n = \langle a,b,c \rangle =$ normal vector and $\langle x,y,z \rangle$ is a point on the plane.

## Scalar forms
$$
\begin{aligned}
a(x-x_0)+b(y-y_0)+c(z-z_0)=0 \\
ax+by+cz=d
\end{aligned}
$$
